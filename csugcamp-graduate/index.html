100% Online Undergraduate and Graduate Programs
CSU-Global coursework was designed with the understanding that you can’t put your life on hold to go back to college. Classes start every four weeks with accelerated 8-week terms, so you can learn with the flexibility and convenience you need.
Affordable, High-Quality Education
We understand the financial commitments of working professionals in today’s economy, which is why we make your degree as affordable as possible. Our tuition rates are lower than most and with our Tuition Guarantee you’ll never see a rate increase.
Designed for Adult Learners
CSU-Global programs are designed for working adults, which means you’ll get the professionalism you know and deserve. Certified instructors all have specialized training in distance learning and online adult education. And classes emphasize theory and application, for a real-world focus to help you hit the ground running after graduation.
Flexible, 24/7 Learning
With a busy work and home schedule, you need flexible learning that never shuts down. CSU-Global coursework is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Learn when and where it’s convenient for you, without the commute and rigid class schedule.
Non-Profit Online University
CSU-Global is a public, state institution with the primary responsibility of providing you a high quality education. As part of the distinguished Colorado State University System, CSU-Global ensures that your education and support are priority one.
