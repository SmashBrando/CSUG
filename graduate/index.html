Master of Healthcare Administration and Management

Master of International Management

About CSU-Global

CSU-Global Campus is the premier provider of innovative, higher learning opportunities for nontraditional students in Colorado and beyond. CSU-Global was the first independent, fully-accredited, 100% online state university in the country, and is part of the renowned Colorado State University System. CSU-Global is regionally accredited and prides its self on providing career relevant bachelor’s and masters degree programs that will help our students be successful in the workplace.
