Why CSU-Global?

Credit Transfer

We accept up to 64 credit hours of junior, community, or career college transfer credit to get you started on your CSU-Global degree.

Tuition Discount and Guarantee

If you are transferring from one of our affiliate community colleges you will also receive a 10% discount on your tuition. Once you enroll at CSU-Global your tuition is locked in and your rate will never increase as long as you stay enrolled.

Support

When you enroll at CSU-Global you'll have a whole support team to help you meet your needs. Whether it's questions about enrollment, student advising, or financial aid, you will have a team of advisors to assist you along the way.

If you attended one of our community college affiliates you could be eligible for additional benefits like our affiliate college scholarship, specialized program to program articulations, and more.

About CSU-Global

CSU-Global Campus is the premier provider of innovative, higher learning opportunities for nontraditional students in Colorado and beyond. CSU-Global Campus is committed to advancing student success in a global society, investing in human capital, expanding the state economy, and enhancing the quality of life for citizens in the state of Colorado and beyond by providing access to dynamic degree programs characterized by academic excellence, innovative delivery technologies, and strong stakeholder engagement.
